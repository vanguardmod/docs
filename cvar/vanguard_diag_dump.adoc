[[cvar.vanguard_diag_dump]]
=== vanguard_diag_dump

[cols="1h,4",frame=all,grid=rows]
|===
| Default       | `0`
| Type          | bool (`0` or `1`, auto-resets to `0`)
| Flags         | none
| Since         | v0.4.3
| Scope         | server
|===

Triggers a one-shot full diagnostic dump on the next eligible shot, including
the capsule diagnostic, bone resolution, and bone-axis world-projection lines
prefixed with `VG_DIAG_DUMP:`.

`vanguard_diag_dump 1`::
  Arms a one-shot dump. After the next eligible shot fires the dump, the
  cvar auto-resets to `0`. The cvar is also auto-armed once whenever
  <<cvar.vanguard_hitbox_debug>> transitions from `0` to `1`.
